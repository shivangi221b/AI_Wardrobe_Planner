Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-07

MisfitAI (“we”, “us”) is an AI wardrobe planner. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account info: basic profile details you provide (e.g., name, email).
  • Optional profile info: birthday and gender if you choose to enter them in-app.
  • Calendar access: if you connect Google Calendar, we request read-only access to fetch event titles/times needed to generate outfit plans.
  • Wardrobe content: images and metadata you upload to build your wardrobe.
  • Usage analytics: limited event data to help us improve the product.

How we use information

  • Provide the core service (wardrobe management and weekly outfit recommendations).
  • Personalize recommendations (optional fields only).
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • Measure and improve performance and usability.

Google user data

If you connect Google Calendar, we use the calendar.readonly scope to read calendar events you can access. We do not request access to your birthday or gender from Google.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us run the app.

Data retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to provide optional profile fields.
  • You can disconnect Google access from your Google Account at any time.
